# Bill Rice – Conference/Podcast/Webinar Introduction

**Bill Rice** is the Founder and CEO of Kaleidico and Principal Consultant of Bill Rice Strategy Group, where he advises fintech companies and mortgage lenders on demand generation and customer acquisition strategy. 

A recognized pioneer in the lead generation industry, Bill coined the term "lead management" and built the first intelligent lead scoring system for mortgage lending. His career includes serving as Employee #7 at DeepGreen Bank—one of the first internet-only banks—and Vice President of National Home Equity at Quicken Loans, where he built the EquityOnline platform.

Before fintech, Bill served as a U.S. Air Force Officer specializing in counterespionage operations and held senior security roles at TASC and Iridium Communications. He holds a B.S. from the U.S. Air Force Academy and an MBA in Marketing from the University of Phoenix.

Today, Bill is an active thought leader on LinkedIn, YouTube, and through his newsletter *My Executive Brief*, where he shares insights on AI-driven marketing, lead generation, and fintech innovation. Please welcome Bill Rice!
