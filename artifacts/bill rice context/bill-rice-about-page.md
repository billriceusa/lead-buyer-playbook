# Bill Rice – Personal Website About Page

## About Bill Rice

I've spent my career at the intersection of technology, strategy, and financial services—building systems, pioneering new approaches, and helping companies grow through smarter marketing and customer acquisition.

### What I Do

I'm the Founder and CEO of **[Kaleidico](https://kaleidico.com)**, a demand generation agency that specializes in helping mortgage lenders and fintech companies scale their customer acquisition. I also run **[Bill Rice Strategy Group](https://billricestrategy.com)**, where I work directly with leadership teams on intensive 120-day go-to-market and growth strategy engagements.

My expertise centers on **lead generation, demand generation, and sales-marketing alignment**—particularly in complex, regulated industries like mortgage lending and financial services. I've built lead management systems from the ground up, developed proprietary frameworks for optimizing conversion rates, and helped dozens of companies refine their approach to customer acquisition.

### The Journey

My path here has been unconventional. I started as a **U.S. Air Force Officer** specializing in counterespionage operations—work that taught me pattern recognition, strategic thinking, and how to analyze complex systems. From there, I moved into technology and national security roles at **TASC** (managing Information Warfare Centers) and **Iridium Communications** (overseeing satellite communications security).

In 2000, I made the leap to fintech as **Employee #7 at DeepGreen Bank**, one of the first internet-only banks. We launched the industry's first unconditional home equity line of credit originated entirely online—groundbreaking at the time. After DeepGreen was acquired by LightYear Capital, I joined **Quicken Loans** (now Rocket Mortgage) as **Vice President of National Home Equity**, where I built and led the **EquityOnline** platform.

In 2005, I founded what would become **Kaleidico**. Initially, we developed **icoSales**—the first intelligent lead scoring and distribution engine for the mortgage industry. Along the way, I **coined the term "lead management"** and contributed the original Wikipedia definition that helped establish it as a distinct discipline. Over nearly two decades, Kaleidico has evolved into a full-service demand generation agency, serving mortgage lenders, fintech platforms, and financial services companies through multiple market cycles.

I've also served as a strategic advisor to companies including **Quizzle** (later sold to Bankrate), **SpringEQ**, and **ProPair**, helping shape growth strategies and product direction during critical phases.

### How I Think About Marketing

Marketing isn't about hacks or tricks—it's about understanding systems, optimizing processes, and aligning incentives. My background in intelligence work gave me a unique lens: I approach lead generation and demand generation like an analyst approaches a strategic problem. It's about identifying patterns, testing hypotheses, and building frameworks that scale.

Whether it's designing a multi-channel acquisition program, building a lead scoring algorithm, or optimizing a sales team's conversion process, I focus on **creating systems that work predictably and improve over time**.
