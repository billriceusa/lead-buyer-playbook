# Bill Rice – About the Author

**Bill Rice** is a leading authority on lead generation, demand generation, and fintech marketing with over 20 years of experience building customer acquisition systems for financial services companies. As Founder and CEO of Kaleidico and Principal Consultant of Bill Rice Strategy Group, he advises mortgage lenders and fintech platforms on scaling growth through strategic marketing and sales optimization.

Rice is recognized as a pioneer in the lead generation industry—he coined the term "lead management" and developed the first intelligent lead scoring and distribution system for mortgage lending. His career includes serving as Employee #7 at DeepGreen Bank (one of the first internet-only banks) and Vice President of National Home Equity at Quicken Loans (now Rocket Mortgage), where he built the EquityOnline platform.

Before entering fintech, Rice served as a U.S. Air Force Officer specializing in counterespionage operations and held senior positions at TASC and Iridium Communications. He holds a B.S. in Political Science from the U.S. Air Force Academy and an MBA in Marketing from the University of Phoenix.

Rice is a regular contributor to industry publications, frequent podcast guest, and speaker at marketing and fintech conferences. He publishes *My Executive Brief*, a weekly newsletter on lead generation and growth strategy, and maintains an active presence on LinkedIn and YouTube. He lives in Monroe, Michigan, where he continues to advise companies and create educational content on AI-driven marketing and demand generation.
